About GynAc

Gynecology Academy is an online learning portal that provides training for skill enhancement in the field of ultrasound imaging for diagnosis of gynecological diseases for practising radiologists, gynecologists, sonologists and students.


Gynecological ultrasound has seen rapid advances owing to an improvement in ultrasound technology, equipment, and expanding research. The literature in this niche area of medicine is, however, limited and scattered. This portal intends to provide comprehensive coverage including recent advances and new-found knowledge in the field of gynecological ultrasound.


The lectures have abundant illustrations of ultrasound features in various pathologies. They include not only two-dimensional grey scale but also Doppler and three-dimensional ultrasound. These lectures also incorporate video clips that capture the interactive nature of transvaginal scanning, which is unique to ultrasound imaging.


Today, ultrasound examination is expected to provide accurate, prompt and site-specific diagnosis of gynecological pathology. The lectures in the portal are designed to achieve this objective.


Online learning is a convenient way of acquiring specific knowledge and expertise in gynecological ultrasound, because it can be done at your own pace, time and place. Gynecology Academy provides this facility.
