Welcome to GynAc


We are glad to launch Gynecology Academy – an online learning portal for skill enhancement and training in the field of ultrasound imaging in Gynecology.


Currently we have three members on the faculty who specialize in this field and more will be joining us soon. In the coming weeks and months many more lectures will be added, which will provide a comprehensive coverage of Gynecological Ultrasound.


The lectures have abundant illustrations of ultrasound features in various pathologies. They include not only two-dimensional grey scale but Doppler and three-dimensional ultrasound as well. These lectures also incorporate video clips that capture the interactive nature of transvaginal scanning, which is unique to ultrasound imaging.


Since this is a new initiative that we have just launched, we would appreciate any feedback that could help us further improve the learning experience. Please email us at feedback@gynac.org
